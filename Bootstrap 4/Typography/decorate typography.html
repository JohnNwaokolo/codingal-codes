Cricket

is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players each. The game is typically played on a grass field with a rectangular 22-yard pitch at the center. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team by
 batting and bowling
. In batting, players aim to hit the ball and score runs by running between wickets or hitting boundaries (four or six runs). Bowlers aim to dismiss batsmen by various means, including striking the stumps or getting them caught. Cricket has formatsrng from short, fast-paced Twenty20 games to longer Test matches lasting up to five days. It is widely followed in countries like
India, Australia, England, and South Africa.
